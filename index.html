The 6 Part Process Includes

1. Customer Profile

Wouldn’t you like to know who’s looking for your products or services? Identify your ideal client and where to reach them online. Even if you think you know your client, this part shows you what you don’t know (yet!).

2. Customer Journey

The customer journey is an important path to your success. Understand the buyer’s journey in your business. Learn where you may be losing customers and get the tools that’ll keep them engaged with your brand.

3. Content

Creating content that interests your audience is what matters most. There’s no point in delivering information that isn’t engaging or useful. Be tactical in content creation, it’s what builds trust and in time the sale.

4. Touchpoints

Get a clear understanding of all the ways customers can find you online, and how you can integrate each touchpoint to maximise your brand’s reach. Business is all about connecting with the right people, in the right place–at the right time!

5. Data

It’s the secret weapon of digital marketing. Data will give you an honest insight into what works in your biz and what doesn’t. It’ll show you where you’re leaving money on the table and what marketing initiatives are engaging your audience.

6. Systems

Yes, there are a gazillion apps and platforms out there. That means more time to focus on the things that you do best, and the things you love outside of business.
